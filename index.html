Welcome To Harber University

Harber University was founded in 1877. It is the oldest university not only in Sweden but in all the Nordic countries that exist today. Since it was attended by Carl Linnaeus,
it is not surprising that the university has focused on the natural sciences. Today, Uppsala is made up of three disciplinary domains: humanities and social sciences, medicine and pharmacy, and science and technology.
